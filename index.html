Learn More

Beginners

We conduct our entire program in Mandarin, which allows our students to be immersed in a complete Mandarin environment by using Chinese nursery songs, stories, puppet animals and interactive games.
Our students will learn the most common conversations in daily life, which will help them to feel both practical and fun.
Our different curriculums are customized to various ages of students. For example, preschoolers (age 2-5) will be using lots of music and puppets to stimulate their interests in starting this new language.
Elementary kids (age 6-8) will start to learn Chinese Pinyin and some basic words. The customized, immersive language curriculum will include writing and recognizing some basic Chinese characters, comprehension of some basic Chinese idioms/ phrases, and carrying some simple conversations in Mandarin.
Our Mandarin immersion environment will ensure our students can learn the language in the most natural way. Our customized curriculums will guarantee to inspire their passions to learn.

Intermediate

This is suitable for students who want to continue studying Mandarin and learns more about Chinese culture. Past experiences in Mandarin learning are recommended, but not required.
The main learning objective of this course is to provide students with listening comprehension and Mandarin conversation skills.
We will also have tailored curriculums that will enable students to deepen their language skills and understanding of Chinese culture.

Learn More

Advanced

This course will help students achieve the goal of reading and writing Mandarin. Speaking authentic Mandarin means a deeper understanding of language and culture.
Students will improve their reading and writing skills, master new vocabularies, and be familiar with formal and informal vocabularies and sentences.
